ABOUT


Stella Mary's College of Engineering is founded and overseen by the Nova Educational Trust, under the leadership of Dr. Nazareth Charles, a respected business entrepreneur in Kanyakumari District. Dr. Charles, an Engineer by profession, is renowned for his philanthropic efforts aimed at uplifting the less fortunate, particularly women and the underprivileged members of society. Dedicated to creating skilled professionals with integrity, the institution strives to contribute to the nation and industry.

The college provides a nurturing environment conducive to academic excellence, fostering leadership skills, and addressing all aspects essential for a successful career. Stella Mary's boasts cutting-edge infrastructure and is committed to delivering education structured around specific goals, systematically mapping and evaluating students' performance to develop their knowledge and skills.

Stella Mary's College of Engineering adheres to global standards in teaching and research, demonstrating an unwavering commitment to excellence in nurturing innovative and entrepreneurial skills. This dedication has earned the institution accreditation from NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) for the overall institution and NBA (National Board of Accreditation) for the Departments of Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science Engineering within just nine years of its establishment.

The college has maintained an outstanding placement record, providing ample opportunities for students to achieve their career aspirations, including placements at the international level.
