ABOUT ICMTEM 2024


Recent developments in the fields of science, technology and engineering have contributed to an explosion of innovation in a wide variety of industries, including new product development and manufacturing, design and optimisation techniques, renewable energy, embedded technologies, smart grids, robotics, structures and construction, big data, remote sensing and geographic information systems. The prestigious "ICMTEM 2024" conference seeks to provide a superior international platform for academics, researchers, engineers, industry participants, and students from all over the world to exchange experiences and research findings. Energy-effective green technologies are being investigated to provide cost-effective solutions and better outputs across multiple sectors. The purpose of this conference is to explore new technologies and advances in science and engineering for superior engineering solutions by bringing together academics, researchers, and specialists from connected industries in a shared platform. Researchers will use this conference as a forum to showcase the results and discoveries of their work. Participants in this conference are invited from all over the world, including practicing engineers, academicians from various universities and engineering institutes, scientists from R&D institutions, research scholars, and postgraduate students.



Objectives of the Conference


Knowledge Sharing: ICMTEM-24 aims to facilitate knowledge sharing among academicians, researchers, and experts from related industries. It provides a common forum for exploration of emerging technologies and innovations in engineering and science.

Sustainable Development: The conference focuses on sustainable development through digital transformation, innovations, and modernized technologies. It aims to promote global scientific collaborations and community partnerships.

Environmental Responsibility: ICMTEM-24 recognizes the importance of environmental responsibility in modern business sectors.

Economic Efficiency: The conference explores economic efficiency in various domains.

Social Solidarity: ICMTEM-24 encourages social solidarity and responsible actions.

Digital Transformations: The achievement of sustainable development through digital transformation is a key theme.

Researchers, practicing engineers, academicians, scientists, and postgraduate students from all over the globe are invited to participate in this conference. It provides a platform to present research findings, discuss innovations, and foster collaboration.

ABOUT COLLEGE


Stella Mary’s College of Engineering is promoted by the Nova Educational Trust & established by the well-known business entrepreneur of Kanyakumari District, Dr. Nazareth Charles. The college is located amidst a beautiful eco-friendly atmosphere surrounded by coconut groves.
