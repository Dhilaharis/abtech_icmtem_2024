ORAL PRESENTER

As an oral presenter, you have to successfully interact with the audience in a live presentation while also facilitating fruitful discussions. For oral presenters, the registration price packages (Registration Link) offer numerous advantages.


Guidelines for Presentation

★ Think about the order and relevancy of your slides. The path from one slide to the next should be constructed.

★ Make utilisation of charts and graphs to highlight your main points. They will aid with the audience's comprehension of the content.

★ Make it easy. The viewer’s get confused when there are too many complex graphs and charts with a lot of data and figures. Avoid using gif graphics, flash, and vibrant colours. Not your message, but only those effects will stick with the viewers. Make it easy!

★ Use the 6-6-6 rule: (maximum 6 words per bullet, maximum 6 bullets per slide, and maximum 6 text slides in a row). The fewest words with effective imagery will have the most powerful effect.

★ Choose easy-to-read fonts with strong contrast that are compatible with most computers. Italics, ALL CAPS, and other distracting and congested additions should not be used. A minimum font size of 30 points is a righteous guidelines.


Presenting Effectively

Make your presentation effective and keep the audience interested throughout your presentation.
